FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this campaign for?

It is for parents, carers, and home owners who want to help children build simple energy-saving habits at home.

What age should parents start?

Children can start learning from around ages 6-7 with simple reminders like turning off lights and closing doors.

What is the main message?

Energy-saving habits start at home, and parents can teach them through everyday routines rather than long lectures.
