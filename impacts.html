Topic 02

How wasted energy affects our future

Leaving lights, fans, chargers, and screens running may seem small, but children notice when waste is accepted at home. Those habits can follow them into school, work, and their own families later.

PSA message

Parents can show children that saving energy is not about fear. It is about respect, discipline, and thinking before using more than we need.
