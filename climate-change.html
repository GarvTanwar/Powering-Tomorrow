Topic 01

Why energy habits matter at home

Children learn from the routines around them. When parents treat electricity as something valuable, kids begin to understand that lights, screens, heating, and cooling should be used with care.

PSA message

Teaching energy-saving habits early makes responsibility feel normal, not like a rule children only hear about later.
