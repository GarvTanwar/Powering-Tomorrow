Who is Powering Tomorrow anyway?

Powering Tomorrow is a student PSA campaign helping families turn energy saving, climate awareness, and sustainable living into simple habits at home.

Public Service Announcement Campaign visual Podcast Listen and learn Physical Artifact Hands-on message

The Powering Tomorrow Story

Small actions at home can grow into lifelong habits.

Our Mission

Our mission is to encourage families to reduce wasted energy, understand climate responsibility, and build daily sustainable habits at home. The campaign focuses on realistic household routines that children can see, understand, and repeat with support from parents and carers.

Why We Started

Climate change and energy bills can feel overwhelming for families. Powering Tomorrow was created to make sustainability practical, calm, and easy for children and parents to understand.

Our Core Values

Five ideas power the campaign.

Instead of a long list of topics, our campaign is built around values that make energy education more useful for families.

Our Approach

A calmer way to learn about energy.

Powering Tomorrow uses simple language, visual learning, practical household routines, child-friendly examples, and calm, positive messaging. The aim is to make sustainability feel like something families can understand and repeat, not something they have to decode.

01

Simple language

Short, clear wording helps families understand energy habits without technical language.

02

Visual learning

Images and campaign visuals make the message easier for children to notice and remember.

03

Household routines

The campaign connects sustainability to everyday actions like lights, devices, heating, and cooling.

04

Child-friendly examples

Examples are written so parents can explain them to children in a natural, age-appropriate way.

05

Positive messaging

The tone stays hopeful, practical, and calm so families feel encouraged to take action.
