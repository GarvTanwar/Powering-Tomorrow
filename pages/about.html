About Us

About This Campaign

Energy Habits at Home is a public service campaign for parents and home owners who want to teach children simple ways to reduce wasted energy.

Our purpose

We focus on everyday family routines: switching off lights, unplugging chargers, checking devices, and helping children understand why these habits matter at home.

Who it is for

The campaign is written for parents, carers, and families. It keeps the message practical, calm, and easy to use with children at different ages.
