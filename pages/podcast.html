Episode description

This episode introduces parents to simple ways they can help children save energy and understand why it matters. It uses a warm conversational tone, one interview voice, and practical examples from everyday home routines.

Topic focus: how parents can turn switching off lights, unplugging chargers, checking devices, and talking about electricity use into small learning moments for children.
