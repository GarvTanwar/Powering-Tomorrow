Campaign Visual

The campaign visual is made for parents to notice quickly and remember during normal home routines.

PSA campaign image

Image description: a paper megaphone is displayed on a desk under a warm lamp, suggesting a public message or reminder. The dark green setting connects the visual to energy-saving habits at home.

The visual encourages parents to guide children through small actions like switching off lights, unplugging chargers, and checking devices before leaving a room.
