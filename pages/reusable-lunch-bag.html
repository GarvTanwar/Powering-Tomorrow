Lets Make Reuseable Lunch Bag

Turn old denim into a useful lunch bag and keep one more throwaway bag out of the bin.

Did you know? An average kid can create about 67 pounds of lunch waste in a year.

Before You Start

Requirements

  • 01Parent or guardian permission
  • 02An old pair of jeans
  • 03Scissors
  • 04Needle and thread or a sewing machine
  • 05Ribbon or cord for the drawstring
  • 06Buttons, patches, fabric scraps, and decorations

Build It

Steps

01

Cut the denim tube

Choose one jeans leg and cut a straight section about 14 to 16 inches long. This becomes the main body of the lunch bag.

02

Create the drawstring channel

Fold the top edge down about half an inch. Sew around the fold, leaving small openings so the cord can slide through.

03

Add the cord

Thread a long piece of ribbon or cord through the channel at the top. Pull both ends gently to test the drawstring.

04

Make the base

Cut another piece of fabric slightly larger than the opening at the bottom of the jeans leg. This will close the bag.

05

Sew the bottom closed

Turn the denim inside out, line up the base fabric, and sew the pieces together. Turn it back the right way when finished.

06

Decorate your lunch bag

Add buttons, patches, fabric shapes, or other decorations from home. Add the Captain Wattson keychain to give your lunch bag an energy-hero finish.
