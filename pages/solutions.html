Topic 03

Daily habits families can adopt

Families can build energy-saving routines through simple actions: switching off unused lights, charging devices only when needed, using natural light, and setting clear expectations for shared spaces.

PSA message

Children are more likely to follow a habit when parents explain it calmly and practise it with them every day.
