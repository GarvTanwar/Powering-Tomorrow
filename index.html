Family Energy Habits

Teaching children at different ages

Age 6-7

Learning the basics

Children practise lights off, closed doors, and unplugging chargers with calm parent reminders.

 Age 9-10

Building routine

Kids start checking switches and helping with bedtime or leaving-home energy routines.

 Age 12-13

Understanding impact

Parents explain how wasted electricity affects the home, the power bill, and shared responsibility.

 Age 15-16

Leading by example

Teenagers manage their own devices and influence younger siblings through everyday choices.
